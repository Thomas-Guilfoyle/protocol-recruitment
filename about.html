About Us:

Protocol Recruitment was founded in 2021 with the end goal of providing an independent and highly responsive recruitment agency in the Midlands area that is focused on people.

As a company, we believe that our vision is simple: We aim to be the best at what we do every day. Our gift is finding your gift!

In the early days of our company, we came up with a list of values that we hope will be around for a long time:

  • Solutions oriented: Solutions are the focus - we solve problems with creativity and determination.

  • Respect for others: Customers and colleagues feel valued when working with us, and each and every client we work with is valued. We respect everyone's needs and feelings, and value every relationship.

  • Honesty and integrity: Transparency and openness are central to everything we do.

  • Winning mentality: When we win, our customers win, it is that simple! Our competitive advantage inspires others to exceed expectations.

  • Flexible approach: Our clients' needs constantly change, and we are dedicated to meeting and exceeding their needs by operating 24/7

  • Fun: In our workplace, we encourage and foster a hardworking yet enjoyable culture; when our employees are happy, so are our customers.

Because of these core values and mentality, we are always the first choice for both clients and employers.
